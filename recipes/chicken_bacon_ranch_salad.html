Chicken Bacon Ranch Salad

Description

Ingredients

  • 8 leaves romaine lettuce
  • 2 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • ½ cup chopped cooked chicken
  • ½ cup chopped avocado
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 slices cooked bacon, crumbled

Directions

  1. Line a salad plate with small Romaine lettuce leaves.
  2. Combine chopped romaine, chicken, avocado, and tomatoes in a large bowl. Add ranch dressing, salt, and pepper, and toss to combine.
  3. Serve salad on the prepared plate and top with crumbled bacon.
